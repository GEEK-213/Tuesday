Summary of PM Modi's Recent Visit to the UAE:

Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s recent visit to Abu Dhabi centered on strengthening the strategic and economic partnership between India and the UAE. The visit was marked by a significant breakthrough, with the UAE committing a $5-billion investment into India. 

Key highlights of the visit include:
- Economic Cooperation: The $5-billion investment is aimed at key sectors including energy, technology, and trade.
- Strategic Partnership: Both nations emphasized the importance of deeper strategic collaboration to address regional challenges.
- Security Dialogue: PM Modi and UAE officials discussed joint security measures in light of recent geopolitical developments.

This visit further solidifies the UAE's position as one of India's most critical strategic partners in the region.
